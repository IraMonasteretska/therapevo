My Approach

I know reaching out for help can be hard to do. I am here to meet you as you are and walk with you in this process. It is important for me to engage with you from a place of curiosity, not judgment, and to support your autonomy.

I am a trauma-informed therapist who is trained in trauma therapy using narrative therapy and emotionally focused therapy. I also use some tools from the Gottman Method and cognitive behavioural therapy. I also approach therapy from both a systems and an attachment perspective.
