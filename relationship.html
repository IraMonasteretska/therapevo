How it Works

At Therapevo, we provide a personalized and proven approach to relationship counseling. Unlike generalized platforms, our family-led team of licensed therapists specializes in helping couples and individuals rebuild, renew, and recover their relationships through evidence-based methods.

In a nutshell, we help you move from a negative, harmful cycle into a positive, nurturing and secure cycle that only increases the safety and connection you feel over time. We start by understanding your unique story through assessing your relational dynamic in our first sessions together. From there, we guide you through a structured, three-step process:

01

Understand Relational Patterns

Identify cycles, emotions, and underlying needs that shape your relationship.

02

Rebuild Emotional Connection

Rediscover and express your emotional needs and work together to foster acceptance and engagement.

03

Create Lasting Change

Develop practical solutions to longstanding challenges and build new ways of relating based on trust and care—equipping you with tools to sustain your progress long after counseling ends.
