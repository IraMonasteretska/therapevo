Betrayal Trauma Therapy

Find Safety, Rebuild Trust, and Establish Healthy Boundaries

Discovering a betrayal can feel like your world has shattered. Whether it’s infidelity, financial secrecy, or a hidden addiction, the hurt and confusion can leave you feeling lost and unsure of what’s next. Our expert therapists provide a secure, compassionate space to help you process the pain, rebuild your confidence, and decide the next steps for your life or marriage.

Schedule a Session to Begin Healing

What is
Betrayal Trauma?

When trust is broken, it’s not just your relationship that suffers—it’s your sense of self and safety. Common sources of betrayal include:

  • Infidelity: Emotional or physical affairs.
  • Financial Secrecy: Hidden debts, gambling, or poor investments.
  • Addiction: Secretive substance use, pornography, or compulsive behaviors.
  • Long-Held Secrets: Uncovering life-altering truths.

These experiences can shatter your view of yourself, your life partner, or even of the safety and predictability of the world around you. This may leave traumatic emotional wounds that are challenging to navigate on your own, but healing is possible with the right support.

The Impact
of Betrayal Trauma

Betrayal doesn’t just affect your relationship —
it impacts every part of your life.

You might notice:

Symptoms like PTSD

Flashbacks, rage, hypervigilance, or feeling numb and disconnected

Loss of Trust

Difficulty believing in others—or yourself

Self-Doubt

Questioning your decisions, worth,
and instincts

Isolation

Pulling back from friends, family
and community

You didn’t sign up for betrayal, but you don’t have to let it define your future.

How We Help the Betrayed

Our therapy for the betrayed is centered on helping you heal, rebuild, and feel whole again.
We’ll work together to:

1

Create Stability

Find emotional and physical safety in your life

2

Process the Pain

Gently explore and understand the nature and effects of the betrayal

3

Set Boundaries

Build clear, healthy limits that protect your well-being

4

Ask for the Truth

Learn what facts you need to know, and what details may be counter-productive to healing

Regain Confidence

Reconnect with your inner strength and sense of self
