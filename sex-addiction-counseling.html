What is Sex Addiction?

Sex addiction goes beyond normal expressions of sexuality, leading to behaviors that feel uncontrollable and harmful. Signs include:

  • Difficulty controlling sexual urges or behaviors
  • Preoccupation with sexual thoughts
  • Struggling to stop despite negative consequences to your health, relationships, or finances
  • Escalating behaviors to riskier or more extreme actions
  • Feeling disconnected from relationships or intimacy
  • Hiding behaviors out of shame or fear

You are more than your addiction, and healing is possible.
