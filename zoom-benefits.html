Therapevo Zoom Benefits

What Is Online
Counseling Like?

At Therapevo, we’ve been offering online therapy since 2010, long before it became the norm. We know how important it is to feel connected to your therapist, even when you’re not in the same room. 

That’s why we hire therapists who work hard to make our clients feel welcome, comfortable and safe to share difficult stories. And as our therapists do so through Zoom, they connect with you face-to-face so that you can move through your story together in real time.

Does It Work as Well
as In-Person Therapy?

Absolutely! Research shows that virtual counseling can be just as effective as in-person therapy for many people. Whether you’re seeking help with relationships, trauma, or personal growth, Zoom video counseling provides the same high-quality care you’d expect from face-to-face sessions, with a few extra perks:

The Benefits
of Zoom Video Counseling

Convenience

Attend sessions from the comfort of your home, office, or anywhere you feel safe and focused. 

Location

Need to move or travel? It doesn’t matter. Working on yourself doesn't mean you have to stay in one place.

Flexibility

Save time and eliminate the stress of commuting to a therapist’s office.

Privacy

Enjoy confidential sessions in your own secure environment.

Accessibility

Connect with specialized therapists regardless of your location.

Effectiveness

Evidence shows that online counselling is just as effective as in-person therapy.
