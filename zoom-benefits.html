Therapevo Zoom Benefits

What Is Online
Counseling Like?

At Therapevo, we’ve been offering online therapy since 2010, long before it became the norm. We know how important it is to feel connected to your therapist, even when you’re not in the same room. 

That’s why we hire therapists who work hard to make our clients feel welcome, comfortable and safe to share difficult stories. And as our therapists do so through Zoom, they connect with you face-to-face so that you can move through your story together in real time.

Does It Work as Well
as In-Person Therapy?

Absolutely! Research shows that virtual counseling can be just as effective as in-person therapy for many people. Whether you’re seeking help with relationships, trauma, or personal growth, Zoom video counseling provides the same high-quality care you’d expect from face-to-face sessions, with a few extra perks:

Why Zoom Video Counseling Is Superior to Text-Based Therapy

While text-based therapy has gained popularity, especially by big box providers, it lacks many elements critical to effective counseling. Here’s why we believe real-time video counseling is a far better choice:

Evidence-Based Methods

Unlike text-based therapy, video counseling is supported by extensive research showing its effectiveness for a wide range of concerns.

Nonverbal Communication

Therapists rely on important cues like body language, facial expressions, and voice tone to fully understand and connect with clients—elements that are missing in text communication.

Clearer Communication

Texting can easily lead to misunderstandings or incomplete insights, as written words often lack nuance.

Timely Responses

With Zoom counseling, you receive real-time interaction and support rather than delayed exchanges that may not meet urgent needs.

At Therapevo, we prioritize connection and results, which is why our online therapy is always conducted through secure, face-to-face video sessions.

What to Expect in a Zoom Counseling Session

Your Zoom therapy session will feel a lot like an in-person meeting, with just a few adjustments for the virtual setting:

01

Secure Connection

Your therapist will send you a private link to join your session. You just need a stable internet connection.

02

Personalized Space

Choose a quiet, comfortable spot where you can speak freely without interruptions or being overheard.

03

Engagement

Using video and audio, you’ll have real-time conversations and relevant approaches tailored to your goals.

How to Prepare for Your Session

1

Test Your Technology

Make sure your internet connection, camera,
and microphone are working.

2

Create
a Distraction-Free Zone

Find a private, comfortable space
where you won’t be interrupted or overheard.

3

Bring What You Need
