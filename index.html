Family-Run, People-Focused Care

A Personal Touch in Mental Health Care

At Therapevo, you’re not just working with a company—you’re connecting with a family. Founded and run by two therapists, Caleb and Verlynda, a husband-and-wife team with a shared passion for relationships and mental health, our mid-sized practice reflects a personal commitment to helping individuals, couples and families thrive.

Unlike large, impersonal therapy corporations, we bring a human touch to everything we do. Our values of compassion, empathy, and personalized care are the foundation of our approach. We believe in fostering meaningful connections, ensuring every client feels heard and supported.

When you choose Therapevo, you’re not just choosing expert therapy—you’re choosing a team that truly cares about your journey.
