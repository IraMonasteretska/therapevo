Who We Are and Why We Care

Find Your Path to
Growth and Well-Being

Welcome to Therapevo! We provide compassionate, expert counseling tailored to the unique needs of individuals, couples, and families. Our mission is to empower you to overcome life’s challenges, reconnect with what matters most, and create a fulfilling, balanced life.

Rooted in empathy and proven expertise, our approach offers personalized support for personal growth, mental health, and relationships. Whatever your journey, we’re here to guide you every step of the way.

Who We Are

Therapevo is a family-led counseling agency, founded and operated by husband-and-wife team Caleb and Verlynda Simonyi-Gindele. Together, we bring a deeply personal and compassionate approach to counseling, combining years of professional expertise with a shared passion for helping others.

Caleb Simonyi-Gindele

As a Registered Clinical Counsellor with the BC Association of Clinical Counsellors and a Certified Sex Addiction Therapist, Caleb has over a 15 years’ experience guiding individuals, couples, and families through life’s toughest challenges.

Verlynda Simonyi-Gindele

Verlynda holds a Master of Arts in Marriage and Family Therapy and is an Associate Member of the CACFT. She specializes in couples counseling, trauma, betrayal and infidelity, and supporting adolescents and young adults. With advanced training in Emotionally Focused Therapy and Brainspotting, Verlynda creates a safe and empathetic space for clients to explore their path to healing and growth.

As co-founders, we’ve built Therapevo on the belief that counseling should be accessible, personalized, and grounded in empathy, provided by true experts. Over nearly 25 years of marriage, we’ve navigated our own share of challenges, which has fueled our dedication to helping others thrive.

Our Philosophy

At Therapevo, we are committed to providing exceptional, professional care that empowers every individual to heal, grow, and thrive. Our approach is rooted in:

  • Proven and Effective Care: We use research-backed methods to ensure meaningful, lasting results.
  • Professional Excellence: Our therapists are highly trained, continuously pursuing education in their specialties, and regularly seeking supervision to provide the best possible care.
  • Compassionate Connection: We treat every client as a unique and valuable individual, creating a safe space for healing and personal growth.

From the comfort of your home, our secure and flexible services are designed to meet you where you are in your journey.

What We Stand For

Life can feel overwhelming, but you don’t have to face it alone. Whether you’re seeking clarity, connection, or healing, our team is here to support you in achieving your goals and building a fulfilling, balanced life. At Therapevo, we recognize that faith can play a meaningful role in the healing journey. Our practice is faith-friendly, and we offer counselors who are experienced in working from a Christian worldview for those who wish to integrate faith into their therapeutic process.

No matter your background or beliefs, we’re dedicated to providing compassionate, professional care that respects and honors your unique story.

More About Us

We’re based in rural Saskatchewan, Canada, but our online services connect us with clients across North America as well as diplomatic, military and NGO personnel serving overseas. Our peaceful surroundings inspire us to bring calm, clarity, and care to every interaction.

While our own lives are not without challenges, we’ve seen firsthand how the right tools, mindset, and support can transform even the toughest situations into opportunities for growth.

I don’t want to idealize our marriage. We’ve been married since 1999, and still have our moments of frustration, anger and offence. You have no idea how frustrating it is to be a subject matter expert on marriage and still have those “Duh!” husband moments! But, somehow, I do manage!
