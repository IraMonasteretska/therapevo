We're Hiring

Join our mission to transform lives and relationships
through expert, compassionate care.

Are you passionate about helping individuals, couples, and families overcome challenges like conflict, betrayal, addiction, and trauma?

Do you thrive on empowering people to rebuild connections and create lasting change?

If so, we’d love to hear from you

Current Opportunity

We’re seeking a Master’s-level, licensed therapist to join our team on a contract basis. Part- or full-time applicants are both welcome.

We also welcome Interns who are seeking to grow their skills in a supportive, collaborative environment.

At Therapevo, we provide the resources, supervision, and opportunities you need to continue your education and thrive while focusing on the work you love—helping clients on their journey to healing and growth

What We’re Looking For

Minimum requirements:

Canadian resident

A Master’s degree in counselling

Registration with a provincial or national governing body

Insured for $2 million or more

Minimum requirements:

Qualifications: A degree in Marriage and Family Therapy (or equivalent) with a systemic approach.

Niched Expertise

We’re looking for therapists with specialized training and
experience aligned with our core practice, this includes:

Relationship Counseling

Couples/Marriage Therapy:

  • Expertise in general couples counseling, with an option to specialize in Christian-focused therapy.
  • Candidates willing to complete this training within six months of starting are also encouraged to apply.
  • Candidates with experience in "Marriage" Counseling are encouraged to apply, as it remains a core service.

Betrayal Trauma & Infidelity Therapy:

  • Ability to guide both betrayers and betrayed individuals through recovery, with a nuanced understanding of codependency as a secondary focus.

Domestic Abuse Counseling:

  • Skills to support individuals in abusive relationships and address the complex dynamics involved.

Addiction Therapy:

  • Porn Addiction Therapy
  • Sex Addiction Therapy
  • Other Behavioral Addictions

Trauma Therapy

Specialized training or experience in
trauma-focused modalities to address:

  • Childhood sexual abuse
  • Spiritual abuse or clergy sexual abuse
  • Domestic abuse trauma
  • Narcissistic abuse
  • Health or medical trauma
  • PTSD

Teen & Family Therapy

Experience working with teenagers or families to address mental health and relational challenges in these unique dynamics.

Other Specialized Therapies

Training or expertise in any of the following areas is highly valued:

  • Spiritual issue
  • Grief, loss, and bereavement
  • Perinatal and postnatal mental health
  • Chronic pain management

Availability:

The ability to work 20–25 client hours per week within a few months of onboarding.

Flexibility:

Comfort with technology and managing a fully virtual schedule.

Collaboration:

A team-oriented attitude and commitment to high-quality care.

Our Clients

You’ll have the opportunity to work with a diverse client base and choose from areas such as:

  • Individuals dealing with pornography or sex addiction
  • Partners impacted by betrayal trauma
  • Couples navigating conflict, trauma, or attachment challenges
  • Families coping with grief, infertility, military distance, or mental health concerns
  • Clients from a wide variety of backgrounds, values, and experiences, but over 1/2 our clients are of the Christian faith

What We Offer

Here’s why therapists love working with us:

Collaborative Team Environment:

Weekly group consultations with our supportive Clinical Director and team.

Flexibility:

Enjoy the flexibility of setting your own schedule, making this role ideal for maintaining a healthy work-life balance.

Streamlined Operations:

Focus on counseling without worrying about admin work—our systems handle client scheduling, payments, and paperwork.

Training and Support:

We provide training on our systems and techniques, including adapting in-room skills for online
settings (even EMDR!).

Transparent Pay:

Competitive rates with no overhead costs and clear remuneration practices.

Focus on Counseling:

Minimal administrative burden so you can dedicate your time to your clients.
