What is Trauma?

Trauma is more than a painful memory—it’s an overwhelming experience that can disrupt your emotions, behaviors, and relationships. It may stem from one specific moment, or many cumulative times that exceeded your capacity to cope:

  • Adverse childhood experiences
  • Betrayal or infidelity
  • Abuse or neglect
  • Accidents, hospitalization, or illness
  • War, violence, or loss

Trauma can leave you feeling stuck, disconnected, or unable to fully engage with life. But healing is possible.

The Impact of Trauma

Trauma doesn’t just stay in the past or heal with the passing of time—it shows up in how we live, love, and connect.

You might notice:

Feeling triggered or overwhelmed by small events

Struggling with trust or emotional connection

Avoiding situations that feel unsafe even though in your brain you know they’re quite safe

Nightmares, flashbacks, or emotional numbness; even dissociation

How We Help

Our expert trauma therapy combines evidence-based techniques with a compassionate, personalized approach to help individuals and couples:

1

Understand Your Story

Explore how trauma has shaped your life and relationships.

2

Process the Pain

Work through difficult emotions and memories in a safe space at the right pace, so as not to re-traumatize you or overwhelm your nervous system.

3

Rebuild and Thrive

Develop tools to reconnect with yourself, your partner, and your life.

We specialize in advanced trauma recovery methods, including EMDR, Somatic Experiencing, and Internal Family Systems, adapted for secure, effective online video counseling.

Afraid to Face the Past?

We get it. We are very particular about how we help people heal from past traumatic experiences. You may feel like revisiting those moments could break open a dam of emotions, leaving you a helpless wreck for days or weeks to come. That is why we only place trauma clients with team members who are not just trauma-informed, but trauma-trained.

Our therapists know how to help you process past trauma where it is stored in your body and nervous system in a titrated (measured), cautious manner where you are not re-traumatized or overwhelmed by those very difficult memories.

While our therapists have the tools, you will be the one in the driver’s seat, empowered to set a pace that works for you. We give you full control over the process and honor your autonomy as well as the wisdom of your body in knowing the correct pace for your healing work.
